Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit funds to Swypt?

Open the Swypt app and tap on the deposit button. You'll receive a unique deposit link. Click the link, connect your wallet, select your token and chain, enter the amount, and confirm the transaction.

What blockchains and tokens are supported?

We support Ethereum Mainnet, Arbitrum One, and Solana Mainnet. You can deposit ETH, USDC, and USDT on Ethereum/Arbitrum, or SOL and USDC on Solana.

What wallets can I use?

For Ethereum and Arbitrum: MetaMask, Rainbow, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, and other EVM-compatible wallets. For Solana: Phantom and Backpack wallets.

What is the minimum deposit amount?

The minimum deposit amount is $15 USD equivalent. Make sure you also have enough native tokens (ETH/SOL) in your wallet to cover transaction fees.

How long do deposits take?

Deposits typically arrive in your Swypt app within a few minutes.

Is Swypt custodial?

Swypt provides a non-custodial interface. We do not custody your funds. Deposits are bridged to Hyperliquid, where you maintain control of your assets through your Swypt account.
