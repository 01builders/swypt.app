Privacy Policy

Effective: 16 March 2026

1. Who we are

Controller: Binary Builders AG, contact@binary.builders.

This policy covers the Swypt iOS app and website at swypt.app (the "Services").

2. What Swypt does

Swypt is a non-custodial interface for interacting with third-party blockchain protocols (HyperLiquid). We do not custody user funds. Some features rely on third-party services: Privy (authentication & embedded wallet), MoonPay (fiat on-ramp), and Amplitude (product analytics).

3. Data we collect and why

Email address

Collected when you sign up via Privy (our authentication provider). Used for account authentication and login. Stored by Privy, not on your device—only held in memory during your app session.

Device ID

Collected automatically by Amplitude (our analytics provider). Used to understand app usage patterns. Not linked to your identity. Not used for ad targeting.

Product interaction (usage data)

Events such as screens viewed, trades placed, and features used. Collected by Amplitude for product analytics only. Not linked to your identity.

Coarse location

Inferred from your IP address by Amplitude. We do not request GPS or location permissions. Used to understand regional usage patterns. Not linked to your identity.

Crash data & performance data

May be collected by Apple via standard iOS mechanisms. We do not use any third-party crash reporting SDK. Used to improve app stability.

On-chain activity

Wallet public address, transaction hashes, and balances are public by design on the blockchain. We do not collect KYC documents—if you use MoonPay, they handle KYC directly under their own policy.

4. Legal bases (GDPR)

We process personal data on the following bases:

  • Performance of contract: operating the Service, enabling wallet creation and signing via Privy, processing transactions.
  • Legitimate interests: security, stability, product analytics (balanced against your rights; you may object—see §10).
  • Consent: marketing communications, if any. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation: where required by law (sanctions/export-control checks, lawful requests).

5. Third-party services

Privy (privy.io)

Purpose: user authentication and embedded wallet management. Data shared: email address. Privy acts as our processor under a Data Processing Addendum. Privy Privacy Policy

Amplitude (amplitude.com)

Purpose: product analytics only (no advertising). Data shared: device ID, usage events, coarse location (from IP). Data stored on EU servers (EU data zone). Amplitude Privacy Policy

MoonPay (moonpay.com)

Purpose: fiat-to-crypto deposits (buying USDC). MoonPay is an independent controller and handles its own KYC/AML under its own terms. We receive only minimal transaction-status information (success/failure). Data shared: wallet address. MoonPay Privacy Policy

HyperLiquid (hyperliquid.xyz)

Purpose: cryptocurrency trading (perpetual futures). Data shared: wallet address and trade orders. All blockchain transactions are public by nature.

6. Apple App Tracking Transparency

We do not track users as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework:

  • No IDFA collection
  • No data shared with ad networks or data brokers
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking

App Store privacy labels: all collected data types (email, coarse location, device ID, product interaction, crash data, performance data) are reported as not linked to user identity and not used for tracking.

7. Data storage

  • No personal data is stored locally on your device. Local storage (UserDefaults) holds only login state, favorite coins, and UI preferences—no PII.
  • Analytics data is sent to Amplitude EU servers.
  • Authentication data is managed by Privy.
  • We do not maintain our own user database.

8. Data retention

  • Email: retained by Privy as long as your account exists.
  • Analytics: retained per Amplitude's data retention policy.
  • On-chain records are immutable and public; we cannot alter or delete them.

9. Cookies & similar tech

We use essential cookies for security and core functionality on our website. If we add analytics or marketing cookies, we will present a consent banner and allow you to change preferences at any time.

10. International transfers

Amplitude data is stored in the EU. Where data is processed outside the EEA/UK/Switzerland, we use EU Standard Contractual Clauses (and any Swiss/UK addenda) to safeguard transfers.

11. Your rights

Under GDPR and applicable law, you may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to processing, and data portability, and to withdraw consent at any time.

Contact contact@binary.builders to exercise your rights. Since we use third-party processors, deletion requests may also need to be directed to Privy and Amplitude.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

12. Children

The Services are not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

13. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate to the risk (access controls, encryption in transit, least-privilege). No system is 100% secure; keep your device and wallet credentials safe.

14. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance.
