Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 August 2025

1. Who we are

Controller: Binary Builders, Bahnhofstrasse 20, 6300 Zug, CH. This policy covers our website and apps (the "Services").

2. What we do (summary)

We provide a non-custodial interface that helps users interact with third-party blockchain protocols (Hyperliquid). We do not custody user funds. Some features rely on third parties like Privy (wallet) and MoonPay (fiat on-ramp).

3. Personal data we process

  • You provide: email/support messages; optional profile details you submit.
  • Automatically collected: device/OS, IP address, general location (country), app/version, diagnostics and crash logs.
  • On-chain activity: wallet public address, transaction hashes, and balances (public by design).
  • We do not collect KYC documents. If you use MoonPay, they collect KYC directly (see §8).

4. Purposes & legal bases (GDPR / nFADP)

We process personal data only for the reasons below and on the stated legal bases. When we rely on legitimate interests, we balance our interests against your rights and you may object (see §10).

Running and securing the Service:

We use limited technical data (for example, device/OS info, IP address, and country) to operate the Interface, prevent fraud and abuse, and keep it reliable. Our legal bases are performance of our contract with you and our legitimate interests in security and stability.

Wallet creation and signing (Privy):

If you choose to create or use a wallet through the Interface, we process only what's needed to enable wallet creation, key management, and transaction signing via Privy, which acts as our processor (see §7). The legal basis is performance of our contract.

Fiat on-ramp (MoonPay):

If you start a MoonPay flow, MoonPay collects and processes its own data (including any required KYC/AML) as an independent controller under its own terms (see §8). We receive only minimal status information to complete the flow.

Communications:

We send essential service messages and respond to support requests. The legal bases are performance of our contract and our legitimate interests in providing customer support.

Marketing (optional):

We send marketing communications or use non-essential analytics only if you consent. You can withdraw consent at any time via the unsubscribe link or your settings.

Legal and compliance:

We may process data to meet legal requirements (for example, sanctions/export-control checks or lawful requests) and to protect our rights and users. The legal bases are legal obligation and legitimate interests.

5. Cookies & similar tech

We use essential cookies for security and core functionality. If we add analytics or marketing cookies, we will present a consent banner and allow you to change preferences at any time.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above, resolving disputes, and meeting legal obligations. On-chain records are immutable and public; we cannot alter or delete them.

7. Sharing & processors

We share personal data with:

  • Privy (wallet technology provider) to enable wallet creation/signing. Privy acts as our processor under a Data Processing Addendum (and, where applicable, SCCs) and processes only as instructed.
  • Hosting, security, and operational vendors (e.g., cloud hosting, error logging).
  • Authorities/third parties where required by law or to protect rights/safety.

We do not sell personal data.

8. MoonPay (independent controller)

If you choose to buy crypto with fiat, the transaction and any required KYC/AML are performed by MoonPay under MoonPay's own Terms and Privacy Policy. MoonPay may share limited transaction-status information with us (e.g., success/failure) but we do not receive your KYC documents.

9. International transfers

We may process data outside your country. Where required, we use EU Standard Contractual Clauses (and any Swiss/UK addenda) with our processors to safeguard transfers.

10. Your rights

Depending on where you live (EEA/UK/CH), you may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object, and data portability, and to withdraw consent. Contact contact@binary.builders. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

11. Children

The Services are not for under-18s, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate to the risk (access controls, encryption in transit, least-privilege). No system is 100% secure; keep your device and wallet credentials safe.

13. Changes

If we materially change this policy, we will post the update and, where required, obtain renewed consent.
