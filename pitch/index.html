  3. Buy crypto on a CEX

  7. Connect wallet to DEX

~45 min · 80% drop-off

  1. Download & open app
  2. Sign up with email
  3. Deposit funds
  4. Start trading

WHY ON-CHAIN

Why This Can't Be Built Off-Chain.

Global Self-Custody

Users own their keys. No geographic restrictions, no custodial risk. Available to anyone with a phone.

Unlike Robinhood, Swypt can't freeze your assets.

Permissionless Interface Layer

Hyperliquid is an open protocol. Anyone can build on it — no partnership deals, no API approval, no revenue share gates.

We ship features the moment they exist on-chain.

Programmable Clan Incentives

On-chain referral and revenue-sharing logic baked into smart contracts. Transparent, instant, trustless payouts.

Clan leaders earn real yield from their community's activity.
