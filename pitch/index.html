WHY ON-CHAIN

Why This Can't Be Built Off-Chain.

Global Self-Custody

Users own their keys. No custodial risk. No KYC. Available to anyone with a phone.

Unlike Robinhood, Swypt can't freeze your assets.

Permissionless Interface Layer

Zero custody liability. Near-zero marginal cost at scale. Hyperliquid handles settlement, we handle the experience.

Smart integrations. Smart product. No risk.

Programmable Clan Incentives

On-chain referral and revenue-sharing logic baked into smart contracts. Transparent, instant, trustless payouts.

Clan leaders earn real yield from their community's volume.
