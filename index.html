Got questions?

We have the answers. If we missed yours, hit us up on X or Telegram.

Swypt is a mobile trading app that lets you trade crypto (spot + leverage) and bet on prediction markets — all in one self-custodial app. Think Robinhood, but onchain.

It means YOU hold your crypto, not us. Your assets live in a wallet that only you control. If Swypt went offline tomorrow, your funds are still yours. Unlike Coinbase or Binance, we never hold your keys.

Prediction markets let you bet on the outcome of real events — elections, sports, crypto milestones, pop culture moments. If you think BTC will hit $150K, you can put money on it. If you're right, you earn.

Your wallet is secured by your device's biometrics and encrypted locally. Since Swypt is self-custodial, there's no central honeypot for hackers — your assets are distributed across individual wallets, not sitting in our vault.

Crypto spot trading (BTC, ETH, SOL, and 200+ tokens), leverage trading (up to 20x on major pairs), and prediction markets (hundreds of live events across politics, sports, crypto, and culture).

Apple Pay, Google Pay, debit/credit card, or bank transfer. Funds arrive in your self-custodial wallet instantly — no 3-5 business day waiting game.
